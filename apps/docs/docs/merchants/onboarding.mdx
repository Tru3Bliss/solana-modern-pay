## Install Solana Pay on Shopify

1. Navigate to the [Shopify Solana Pay App Installation Link](https://apps.shopify.com/solana-pay) on the Shopify App Store and click the “Add app” button.

## Configure Solana Pay Portal

1. Enter in your business information. It can take up to 48 hours to be verified by our team.
2. Enter in your Solana wallet address. This is where you will receive payments from your customers.
3. Review and accept the [Privacy Policy](https://merchant.solanapay.com/privacy) and [Terms of Service](https://merchant.solanapay.com/termsofservice).
